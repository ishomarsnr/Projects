Data Analysis and Visualization for Airbnb Listings

Introduction

This project involves analyzing the New York City Airbnb data to provide insights and recommendations to improve the company's business model and increase revenue. The analysis helps Airbnb make data-driven decisions about pricing, marketing, and customer experience.

Dataset Description

The dataset comprises various features of Airbnb listings, including price, room type, location, and availability. It contains 10,000 entries labeled with details about each listing.

Key Questions

  • What is the average price of Airbnb listings in each neighborhood and how does it vary by room type?
  • Which neighborhoods have the highest and lowest average prices for Airbnb listings?
  • How many listings are there per room type, and how does the price and availability vary by room type?
  • Are there any trends in the popularity of Airbnb listings in different neighborhoods over time?
  • What is the impact of ratings and reviews on the price and popularity of Airbnb listings?
  • What are the top hosts and neighborhoods in terms of customer satisfaction, based on ratings and reviews?

Findings & Insights

1. Average Price of Listings

Entire home/apartment has the highest price average across different neighborhood groups.

2. Highest and Lowest Average Prices

Manhattan has the highest average rate of $200, indicating it is the location with the potential to generate more revenue. The Bronx has the lowest average price. The most expensive neighborhood is Glendale.

3. Listings per Room Type

"Entire apartment" type has the highest count of rooms at 2,500, while shared apartments have the least. It is also the most expensive type of apartment. Shared rooms have the highest average availability of 162 days per year.

4. Popularity Trends

Entire apartments are most popular in Manhattan, while private rooms rank highest in Brooklyn.

Summary of Findings

  • Apartments in Manhattan are priced the highest and remain competitive based on availability.
  • Entire apartments do well in busy locations like Manhattan, while private rooms perform better in Brooklyn.
  • Airbnbs in the Bronx are not performing well compared to other locations, despite being the least expensive.

Recommended Actions

  • Increase marketing efforts to get more listings in Manhattan and the Bronx to boost revenue.
  • Advise hosts to choose the right apartment setup based on location to attract demand.
  • Collaborate with hosts in underperforming neighborhoods like the Bronx to offer discounts and drive traffic.

Conclusion

With the right strategy, Airbnb stands a great chance of boosting its revenue by empowering hosts with the right information and support.
